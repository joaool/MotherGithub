Introducing FrameLink

FrameLink is targeting micro and small businesses. Please check the main features below.

Easy to use

FrameLink was made to be simple for everyone. No need for programming, HTML, Database or user interface skills. No need for specific steps to create your information system. Just follow your intuition, you can always change your mind later without loosing any data.

Get immediate results

Instead of long tutorials teaching you many steps until you see something done, FrameLink allows you to design and see immediate results. Build your own Information System accessible from anywhere and connect to your customers (newsLetters, setting appointments, selling products on line and others).

Do-It-Yourself

With FrameLink you do not need a consultant between you and your business idea. Achieve independence while saving time and money. At anytime you decide and build the next stage of your Information System.
