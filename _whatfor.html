Exactly what does Framelink do ?

We help you to move your business operations to the cloud.

  • Upload your data and access it anywhere and in any device.

  • Add services to your FrameLink database and get a fully functional company backend.

    • What is a FrameLink Database ?

    It is an online database that you fine tune to your business.

  • No need to know any database concepts.

  • Define the data you need using plain English.

    • What Framelink is not

  • Not a site builder - FrameLink is focused in your internal operations (although you can give access to some informations to your business partners).

  • Not a CRM like application - With a CRM like application, you adapt your data to an existing database. With FrameLink, services are adapted to YOUR database.
