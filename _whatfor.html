Exactly what does Framelink do ?

We help you to move your business operations to the cloud.
By uploading your data to a Framelink database, that data will be immediately available to you and your company staff anywhere and in any device. By adding services to your FrameLink database you will get a fully functional business operation system.

What is a FrameLink Database ?

It is an online database that you can fine tune to your business without knowing any database concepts. To adjust the FrameLink database to your business all you need is to define the meaning of your data in plain english.

What Framelink is not

  • FrameLink is not a site builder - FrameLink is focused in your internal operations (although you can give access to some informations to your business partners).

  • Frame Link is not a CRM like application - A CRM or other web applications have a database that you must adapt to. With FrameLink, the services (available in our site by a single click) are adapted to YOUR database.
